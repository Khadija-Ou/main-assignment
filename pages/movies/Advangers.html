The Avangers

is a blockbuster superhero film released in 2012, part of the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU). Directed by Joss Whedon, the movie brings together iconic Marvel superheroes to form a super-team. When Earth faces a threat from the villainous Loki, Thor's adopted brother, a covert government agency known as S.H.I.E.L.D. assembles a group of remarkable individuals: Iron Man (Tony Stark), Captain America (Steve Rogers), Thor, Hulk (Bruce Banner), Black Widow (Natasha Romanoff), and Hawkeye (Clint Barton).
